About

My name is Rémy, I am a Web Lover and I focus myself on Front-end Development and Webdesign.

Since my first years, I always enjoyed building things. I started developing my appetite for creation with Lego, continued with Minecraft and finally discovered Web development. It was a relief to find out what I really wanted to do as a living. I took full advantage of the motivation I had to start a long, passionate but sometimes difficult journey of learning front-end technologies as a self-taught.

I want to thank brilliant and essential platforms in this journey. They have proved to be very qualitative, deep, and almost entirely free:
OpenClassrooms, FreeCodecamp, theOdinProject and CodeCademy.

I now code everyday for a living and a hobby with the powerful trio HTML, CSS and Javascript, as well as some additional frameworks and libraries such as Bootstrap, Sass and jQuery.
You can check my resume Online or Download it.

I invite you to check different projects I realised for a better overview of my skills in the Creations section.
If you have any question, comment or want to see my resume, feel free to reach the Contact section.
